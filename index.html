Wholesale Orders

Wholesale pricing is available for shops, dealers, and bulk buyers.

Wholesale Deposit Policy

A deposit is required at the time of order for all wholesale orders.

  • Deposits are refundable if the order is canceled within 24 hours of placement.
  • Deposits become non-refundable if the order is canceled after 24 hours.
  • After 24 hours, tires may have already been pulled from inventory, reserved, and prepared for your order.

By placing a wholesale order, the customer agrees to the deposit and cancellation policy.

Delivery Information

Delivery is available to GA, AL, MS, FL, NC, SC, and TN for a surcharge.

  • Payment must be made in full at least 24 hours before scheduled delivery.
  • Delivery fees vary by location.
  • Contact us for delivery pricing and availability.
